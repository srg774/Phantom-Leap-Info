About the Game

Developed from an original concept game created a few years ago, this latest Halloween-themed game adds a spooky twist to the platformer genre. Designed for all platforms and ages 8+, it is free and here for your entertainment! Currently, I'm still working through some bugs related to theme music and animation speeds on different versions of Android, but I'm collaborating with beta testers to fine-tune it. Feel free to remix this and reach out if you do, especially those who received the cassettes!

Bonus Points: You are generally aiming for 200 souls or more to unlock the top-tier message, but the more souls, the merrier!

Game Controls

  • Arrow Left or Touch Left: Move player left
  • Arrow Right or Touch Right: Move player right
  • Arrow Up or Spacebar or Touch Anywhere: Jump
